A LITTLE ABOUT OUR COMPANY

The Future is Upon Us

The age of virtual & augmented reality is upon us with advancements in computer vision, graphical computing, and optical technology, but we won't begin to see head mounted displays or smart-glasses being used by individuals on a daily basis for quite some time.

Our Thoughts

We believe that the rise of wearable computing will be slow to market, but will eventually take over our lives in every way, shape, and form. Just like the first iPhone took a few years to gain traction and popularity, so too will wearable devices become a staple of life in the near future.

Mobile is Here To Stay

The smart-phone harnesses an immense amount of technology to make it a perfect host for augmented reality applications. With a low barrier-to-entry and universal adoption, the Android and iOS market will continue to lead the augmented reality revolution.

AUGMENTING YOUR REALITY ONE STEP AT A TIME

Our motto is to reshape the reality of all of our customers and clients. Join us in this revolutionary transition.
