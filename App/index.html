A LITTLE ABOUT OUR COMPANY

We believe that the rise of wearable computing will be slow to market, but will eventually take over our lives in every way, shape, and form. The age of virtual reality is upon us with advancements in computer vision, graphical computing, and optical technology, but we won't begin to see head mounted displays or smart-glasses in everyone's homes for quite some time. Just like the first iPhone took about three years to gain traction and popularity, so too will wearable devices become a staple of life in the near future.

In the mean time, we believe that the smart-phone still provides the right technology to begin making augmented reality applications. With a low barrier-to-entry and universal adoption, the Android and iOS market will continue to lead the augmented reality revolution. Beware of the trap of developing for a specific HMD or brand. There are too many out there to predict who will be declared the winner when the HMD war is over.
